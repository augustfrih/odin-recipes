Egg and tomato salad

Description

A spring salad made with new potatoes, tomatoes and eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Chives
  • Sardines
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs and potatoes.
  2. Cut all the ingredients into pieces
  3. Mix the sardines, olive oil and vinegar into a vinaigrette
  4. Mix everything together
