Sichuan fish

Description

Fish cooked sichuan style

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Packet
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Take 500-600 g of fish and cut into cubes. marinate with starch, salt an xian wine and set aside.
  2. Start boiling your rice
  3. Warm oil in a pan or wok and add the packet
  4. Add fishstock and fish
