Tortellini soup

Description

Tortellinis in egg drop (straciatella) soup

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • Tortellini
  • Eggs
  • Parmeggiano
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Bring the chicken stock to a boil and add the tortellini
  2. Whip the egg together with the parmeggiano and nutmeg
  3. When the tortellini is al dente add the egg mixture to the stock and take of the heat
  4. Enjoy!
